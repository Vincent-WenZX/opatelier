The Beginning

A Vision Born
in Winter

Founded in the quiet of a Parisian winter, Plum was born from a singular observation — that true beauty, like the plum blossom, reveals itself most powerfully against stillness.

Our founder envisioned a house where restraint is not absence, but the highest form of intention.

“

Elegance is not about being noticed. It is about being remembered.

— Founder, Plum Maison
